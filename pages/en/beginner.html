PA:Titans has NO unit limit!

Unlike many RTS games, you can create as many units as you want.
The more you produce = The more you win.
If you have the resources, spam units. An army of 500 units? That's normal. 1000? Even better!

Protect your Commander

It dies = You lose. Period.

‚úÖ Where to place the Commander

  • Behind your lines - Never at the front!
  • Near your Factories - In the heart of your base
  • Under AA cover - Galata or Storm nearby
  • Near an Anti-Nuke - In late game
  • With an Umbrella - Against orbital lasers

‚ùå Fatal mistakes

  • On the front line - Guaranteed snipe
  • Alone without defense - Easy target
  • Expanding far - Too exposed
  • Without AA nearby - Death by bombers
  • Stationary and visible - Enemy prepares their move
üí°
The Commander can fight!
Your Commander has a powerful cannon (the Uber Cannon with D). It can defend in emergencies, but don't rely on it. Its real value is building, not fighting.

How to Attack

Breaking enemy defenses

You have a big army and the enemy is well defended? Here are several methods:

üèÉ Method 1: Economic Raid

Don't attack their main base. Attack their economy!

  • Send 5-10 Dox to the enemy's isolated Mex
  • Use Bombers to destroy Energy Plants
  • Divide their attention while your main army prepares

üî® Method 2: Massive Push

When you have numerical advantage, crush them.

  • Gather ALL your army (minimum 50+ units)
  • Add AA (Spinners/Storm) to protect against aircraft
  • A-Move (A key) towards their base - your units attack everything on the way
  • Keep producing during the attack - reinforcements arrive!

‚úàÔ∏è Method 3: Air Domination

If the enemy has no AA, the skies are yours.

  • Build an Air Factory then produce Bumblebee (bombers)
  • Scout first to check they don't have Galata/Spinner
  • Target: Fabbers, Energy Plants, Mex - destroy their eco
  • In T2: Kestrel (gunships) destroy everything that moves

üåç Method 4: Multi-Planet Attack

If they're on another planet, here's how to reach them.

  • Simple option: Astraeus + Fabber ‚Üí set up base ‚Üí Teleporter ‚Üí send army
  • Aggressive option: Unit Cannon to spam Dox/Slammer directly at them
  • Nuclear option: Nuke their base (check they don't have Anti-Nuke!)
  • Final option: Halley to crash their planet with a moon üåô

üíÄ Method 5: Commander Snipe

Why destroy their base when you can kill their Commander?

  • Scout to find where their Commander is
  • Prepare 10+ Slammer or 15+ Bombers
  • Use Teleporter or Unit Cannon to drop directly on them
  • Alternative: Nuke their position

Must remember

‚úÖ ALWAYS
